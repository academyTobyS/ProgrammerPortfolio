JO BLOGGS: Sheffield Computer Science Graduate

About Me

I am a Sheffield graduate with a passion for making videogames and a first class degree in Computer Science. I enjoy playing videogames of all kinds of genres, but am a particular fan of Sackboy: A Big Adventure for PS5.

I enjoy spending time outdoors, and am a keen rock climber and open-water swimmer in my free time.

I have taken part in numerous game jams and team development projects and I believe that my ability to learn and communicate technical information makes me an asset to game teams. I enjoy working with other creative disciplines and relish the creative partnerships in gamedev. I enjoy solving technical problems in creative ways and love to learn new things, so I would particularly appreciate the opportunity to work in the ever-changing technological landscape of the video game industry.
